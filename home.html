KIAKA is a cooperative that was founded in 1976 with the aim to create jobs for unemployed youth in former Kanama commune. KIAKA stands for the umbrella of artisans in Kanama commune. Jean Pierre Godding, then a Belgian volunteer in Gisenyi prefecture, was the main architect of the cooperative. From its inception, KIAKA had full support from local authorities of thelocal government of Kanama.
